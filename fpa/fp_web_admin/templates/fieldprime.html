Welcome to FieldPrime

FieldPrime Software

FieldPrime is a software suite designed to ease the process of manual collection and collation of data. Its original use was to collect phenotype data for large field trials of wheat varieties.

The software consists of the FieldPrime Android app - which is used for the data collection - and the FieldPrime Server, which is used to store and manage the trial data.
